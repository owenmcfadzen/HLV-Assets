## Key Features Overview

### Design Innovation

**Liquid Glass:**
A fresh design approach incorporating optical glass qualities and fluid aesthetics across Lock Screen, Home Screen, Control Center, and applications

**Dynamic Time Display:**
Time adapts to photo wallpapers while maintaining subject visibility; includes 3D effects when device moves

**App Icon Customization:**
Users can select from light/dark appearances, color-tinted options, and transparent variants

**Translucent Controls:**
Interface elements reflect surroundings and morph fluidly across applications

---

### Communication Enhancements

**Call Screening:**
Unknown callers are answered automatically; user receives information before deciding to engage

**Unknown Sender Filtering:**
New screening tools detect spam across Messages, Phone, and FaceTime

**Hold Assist:**
Phone app maintains position in customer service queues with agent notifications

**Polls & Backgrounds:**
Group messaging supports voting features and customizable conversation backgrounds

---

## Apple Intelligence Capabilities

**Live Translation:**
Automatic message translation across supported languages in Messages, FaceTime, and Phone applications

**AirPods Integration:**
Spoken translations in natural voices during multi-language conversations

**Visual Intelligence:**
Search across frequently-used apps, calendar integration, and screen-based questioning capabilities

**Image Creation:**
Emoji-description mixing, ChatGPT style options, Genmoji, and Image Playground tools

**Workflow Automation:**
Intelligent Shortcuts can summarize text, generate images, and access AI models

---

## Discovery & Personalization

**Maps Learning:**
System adapts to regular routes and provides proactive delay alerts

**Visited Places:**
Location memory with easy sharing capabilities

**Music Features:**
AutoMix provides DJ-style song transitions; Lyrics Translation supports multiple language pairs

**Digital ID in Wallet:**
U.S. passport integration for TSA checkpoints and in-app authentication

**New Games App:**
Unified game library with friend activity, recommendations, and score competitions

## Device Compatibility

iOS 26 supports iPhone models from iPhone 12 through iPhone 17 Pro Max, including:
- iPhone SE (2nd generation and later)
- iPhone 11 through iPhone 15 series
- Latest iPhone 16 and 17 lineups
