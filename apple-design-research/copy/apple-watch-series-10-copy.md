## Get to Know Apple Watch

### Health
- Detects chronic high blood pressure signs
- Sleep score tracking, sleep apnea alerts
- ECG app capability
- Vitals app for daily health metrics
- Cycle Tracking with temperature sensing

### Fitness
- Activity rings (Move, Exercise, Stand)
- Multiple workout tracking options
- Running-specific features: Heart Rate Zones, Pacer, Custom Workouts
- Water, dust, and crack resistance

### Safety
- Fall and crash detection
- Check In notifications for arrivals
- Find My app integration
- Siren feature (Ultra models)

### Connectivity
- 5G support for cellular models
- Apple Music streaming (100+ million songs)
- Apple Pay functionality
- Apple Cash support
- Wallet integration with transit cards, home keys, car keys

### Personalization
- Interchangeable bands in various styles/colors
- Customizable watch faces
- App Store access with thousands of apps

### Apple Watch for Kids
- Family Setup capability
- Independent connectivity without iPhone
- Call and text functionality
- Location sharing

### Apple Watch + iPhone Integration
- Ping nearby iPhone feature
- Camera remote control
- Turn-by-turn navigation
