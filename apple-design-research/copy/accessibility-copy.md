## Hero Section

**Headline:** Innovation that's accessible by design.

**Body:** The best technology is designed with everyone in mind. That's why our products and services have built-in features to help you create, connect, and do what you love, your way.

## Vision Features

### Magnifier
**Headline:** Zoom in on the details, right from your Mac
- Works as a digital magnifying glass using device cameras
- Supports multiple viewing windows, document reading, and text adjustment
- Available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac

---

## Hearing Features

### Live Listen
**Headline:** Amplify conversations and get captions of what you hear
- Picks up audio from speakers at distance and transmits to headphones
- New Apple Watch integration includes real-time live captions
- Works with AirPods, Beats, and Made for iPhone hearing devices

### AirPods Pro 3 + Hearing Health
- Includes clinical-grade hearing aid feature
- Offers scientifically validated hearing test (~5 minutes)
- Provides active hearing protection across listening modes
- Supports customization of amplification, balance, and tone

---

## Speech & Mobility Features

### Personal Voice
**Headline:** Create a voice that sounds like you
- Uses on-device AI/machine learning for voice generation
- Can be created from short three-word phrases for those with pronunciation difficulties
- Integrates with Live Speech and third-party AAC applications
- Data remains encrypted on-device or can be saved to iCloud

### Eye Tracking
**Headline:** Control your device with just your eyes
- Uses front-facing camera; works without additional hardware
- Supports dwell control and switch control integration
- Keyboard typing and navigation features included
- Available on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Vision Pro

---

## Cognitive Features

### Assistive Access
**Headline:** Customize your apps to work for you
- Designed specifically for people with cognitive disabilities
- Features high-contrast buttons, large text, and visual alternatives
- Customized experiences for Phone, Messages, Camera, Photos, Music, Magnifier
- Supports grid-based or row-based home screen layouts
- Compatible with third-party AAC apps
