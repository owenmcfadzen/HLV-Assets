**Headline:** The best of Apple. All in one.

**Subheading:** Apple One bundles up to six Apple subscriptions for one lower monthly price, including up to 2TB of iCloud+ storage.

## Bundle Tiers & Pricing

### Individual Plan
- **Price:** $19.95/month
- **Savings:** $12/month (31% discount)
- **Storage:** 50GB iCloud+
- **Services:** Apple TV+, Apple Music, Apple Arcade
- **Sharing:** Not included

### Family Plan
- **Price:** $25.95/month
- **Savings:** $14/month (30% discount)
- **Storage:** 200GB iCloud+
- **Services:** Apple TV+, Apple Music, Apple Arcade
- **Sharing:** "up to five people"

### Premier Plan
- **Price:** $37.95/month
- **Savings:** $32/month (43% discount)
- **Storage:** 2TB iCloud+
- **Services:** All six subscriptions (adds Apple Fitness+, Apple News+)
- **Sharing:** "up to five people"
