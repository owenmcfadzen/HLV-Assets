## Technology

### Display System

**Headline:** More pixels than a 4K TV. For each eye.

"The custom micro‑OLED display system features 23 million pixels, delivering stunning resolution and colors. And a specially designed three‑element lens creates the feeling of a display that's everywhere you look."

### Spatial Audio

**Headline:** Our most advanced Spatial Audio system ever.

"Dual-driver audio pods positioned next to each ear deliver personalized sound while letting you hear what's around you. Spatial Audio makes sounds feel like they're coming from your surroundings."

**Audio Ray Tracing:**
"Audio ray tracing analyzes your room's acoustic properties to adapt and match sound to your space."

**Headphone Support:**
"AirPods Pro 3 and AirPods 4 offer the perfect experience — featuring Lossless Audio with ultra-low latency, supported by the H2‑to‑H2 connection across devices."

### Eye Tracking

**Headline:** Responsive, precision eye tracking.

"A high‑performance eye‑tracking system of LEDs and infrared cameras projects invisible light patterns onto each eye. This advanced system provides ultraprecise input."

### Sensor Array

**Headline:** A sophisticated sensor array.

"A pair of high-resolution cameras transmit over one billion pixels per second to the displays so you can see the world around you clearly. The system also helps deliver precise head and hand tracking."

### Chip Performance

**Headline:** Revolutionary dual‑chip performance. Now powered by M5.

"Apple Vision Pro makes a giant leap in chip performance with the new M5. It simultaneously runs visionOS, executes advanced computer vision algorithms, and delivers stunning graphics."

**R1 Chip:**
"The R1 chip is specifically dedicated to process input from the cameras, sensors, and microphones, streaming images to the displays within 12 milliseconds."

**Performance Gains:**
- Enables up to 120Hz refresh rates
- Renders 10% more pixels
- Delivers up to 3 hours video playback
- 10% sharper text and visuals
- Up to 2x faster AI workloads

**Thermal System:**
"A specially designed thermal system gently moves air through Apple Vision Pro, so it can deliver phenomenal performance while remaining cool and quiet."

**Infrared Illuminators:**
"Infrared flood illuminators work with the external sensors to enhance hand‑tracking performance in low‑light conditions."

**Headline:** Designed to make a difference.

**Subheading:** Our values lead the way.

"Apple Vision Pro was designed to help protect your privacy and keep you in control of your data. Its built‑in accessibility features are designed to work the way you do."

**Optic ID:**
"Use Optic ID to authorize purchases and unlock passwords with the uniqueness of your iris. Optic ID data is private and encrypted, and it never leaves your device."
