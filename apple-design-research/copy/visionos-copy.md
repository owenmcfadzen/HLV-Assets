## Key Features & Spatial Computing

### Widgets
"Widgets let you place go-to apps and info in your space — and keep them there." Customize frame thickness, color, and depth for Calendar, Clock, Music, and Reminders.

Transform photos with lifelike depth perception. Available in Photos, Safari, and Spatial Gallery with third-party developer support.

Enhanced facial rendering with improved hair, lashes, and complexion for more natural representations.

### Shared Experiences
"Share Apple Vision Pro experiences with people in the same room" for movies and spatial games together.
