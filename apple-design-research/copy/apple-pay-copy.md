## Hero Section

**Headline:** Pay the Apple way.

**Primary Value Proposition:** Faster and easier than using cards or cash.

---

## Key Payment Features

### Setup & Flexibility
- "Set up in seconds. Right on your iPhone."
- Add cards via Wallet app by tapping card to device
- "Pay in full, over time, or with your card's rewards"
- Split purchases into smaller payments over time
- Redeem card rewards, points, or miles at checkout

### Acceptance & Availability
- Accepted at "over 85 percent of retailers in the U.S."
- Works "on millions of websites and apps"
- Available in stores, online, apps, and through Safari
- "Tap more. Touch less."

### Apple Cash Integration
"Easy and private way to send and receive money in Messages and Wallet"
- Send/request funds in group chats
- Spend Apple Cash in stores, apps, and online
- Add memos to track transactions

---

## Security Features

- "Privacy and security built in"
- Device-specific number + unique transaction code per purchase
- Actual card numbers never shared with merchants or Apple servers
- Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode required for all transactions
- Transaction info not tied back to users
- Hide My Email option for online purchases
