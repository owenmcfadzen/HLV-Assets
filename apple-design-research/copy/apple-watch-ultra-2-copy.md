## Key Features & Capabilities

### Health Monitoring
- Chronic high blood pressure detection via optical sensor
- Sleep score tracking with Wind Down reminders
- Sleep apnea detection notifications
- ECG app functionality (similar to single-lead electrocardiogram)
- Heart rate monitoring with irregular rhythm alerts
- Vitals app for overnight health metrics
- Temperature sensing for cycle tracking

### Fitness & Activity
- Activity rings tracking (Move, Exercise, Stand)
- Multiple workout types with accurate metrics
- Heart Rate Zones and Pacer views
- Custom Workouts with interval training
- Water resistant design for swimming
- Dust and crack resistant construction

### Safety Features
- Fall detection with automatic emergency assistance
- Crash detection notifications
- Check In feature for arrivals/departures
- Find My app integration
- Siren function on Ultra models
- Emergency SOS via satellite (Ultra 3)

### Adventure/Durability (Ultra 3 Specific)
- "Our largest, most advanced Apple Watch display" with enhanced brightness
- Dual-precision GPS ("best GPS in a sports watch")
- Up to 42 hours battery (normal use)
- Up to 72 hours with low power mode
- Built-in satellite communications capability
- Action button for customizable functions
- Suitable for diving, hiking, marathon activities

### Connectivity
- 5G cellular support available
- Apple Music streaming (100+ million songs)
- Apple Pay support
- Apple Cash integration
- Wallet functionality (transit, home key, car key)
- Incoming call/text capabilities

### Personalization
- Multiple band styles and colors available
- Customizable watch faces (Photos, Snoopy, Nike, etc.)
- App Store access with thousands of apps
