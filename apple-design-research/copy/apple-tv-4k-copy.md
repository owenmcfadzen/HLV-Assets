## Entertainment Features

### Apple TV App
- Watch, rent, or buy shows and movies in one curated interface
- Access hundreds of Apple Originals (dramas, sci-fi, comedies)
- Subscribe to individual channels without additional apps or passwords
- Family sharing for up to six members

### Live TV
- Stream live content from major networks, broadcasters, and pay TV providers
- Access sports via ESPN and MLB
- News from ABC, CNN, Bloomberg
- Services like Hulu, YouTube TV, and Sling TV

### InSight Feature
- Get information about actors and music while watching
- Details automatically update as scenes change
- Available through media player controls or iPhone remote

### Apple Fitness+
- Largest library of 4K UHD fitness and wellness content
- 12+ workout types including HIIT, Yoga, Meditation
- Personalized recommendations and custom plans
- Real-time in-session metrics
- New sessions added weekly

### Apple Music
- Over 100 million songs and 30,000 playlists
- **Apple Music Sing:** Sing along with adjustable vocals
- Use iPhone as microphone; audio amplified through TV

## Gaming & Apps

### Apple Arcade
- Immersive gaming on large screen
- Multiuser support tracking individual progress
- Compatible with PlayStation and Xbox Wireless controllers

### Additional Services
- **Photos:** iCloud Shared Photo Library display
- **Screen Savers:** Aerials, Snoopy animations, personal photo memories

## FaceTime & Communication

- Seamless connection from iPhone/iPad to TV via Continuity Camera
- FaceTime Live Captions in multiple languages
- Incoming call notifications on TV screen
- Bigger, clearer video communication

## Audio & Video Quality

### Picture
- 4K resolution with advanced HDR formats
- **Dolby Vision** and **HDR10+** support
- Details visible in shadows and highlights

### Sound
- Spatial Audio with Dolby Atmos, 5.1, or 7.1 surround sound
- HomePod integration for three-dimensional audio
- AirPods compatibility with spatial sound centering
- **Enhance Dialogue:** Pulls spoken words forward for clarity
- Automatic subtitle timing

## Siri Remote

- Touch-enabled clickpad for intuitive navigation
- Dedicated Siri button matching iPhone design
- Voice recognition for up to six family members
- Personalized recommendations by user
- Control TV power, input, and volume
- Ask questions and get answers

**Sample Voice Commands:**
- "What should I watch?"
- "What did she just say?"
- "Show me the babies' room" (smart home access)

## Device Integration

### iPhone
- Multifaceted remote via Lock Screen and Control Center
- Face ID for purchases and app sign-in
- Find Siri Remote functionality

### Color Calibration
- iPhone camera automatically adjusts TV colors for optimal appearance

### Audio Sharing
- Connect two AirPods sets simultaneously in Spatial Audio
- Individual volume control for each listener

### Siri on AirPods
- Hands-free playback control
- Access scores, show recommendations
- Smart home command control

### SharePlay
- Watch movies and TV together in real-time
- Split View shows all participants' reactions
- Requires compatible streaming apps and devices

## Smart Home Control

### Smart Home Hub Features
- Centralized control via Control Center
- HomeKit-enabled accessory support
- Matter protocol compatibility for expanded device ecosystem
- Remote access via Home app on other Apple devices

### Automation
- Schedule accessory actions automatically
- Create multi-accessory scenes (bedtime locks, movie-night dimming)
- Customizable rules by user preference

### Privacy & Security
- Home data stored securely; Apple cannot read it
